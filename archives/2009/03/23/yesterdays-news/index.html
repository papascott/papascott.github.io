Yesterday's News

23 Mar 2009

Yesterday morning we attended a rally in support of a new school in Jesteburg. I got recruited as a drummer, we were at the front of the march, and the drummers ended up in full color in the Hamburger Abendblatt. That's me with the white cap and the grim expression on the far left. The story (without photos) is also on the Abendblatt website.
