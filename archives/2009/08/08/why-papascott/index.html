Why PapaScott?

The oldest remaining shards of what would be become PapaScott date back 10 years to August 1999 and were posted with Blogger, a shiny new technology at the time.

In 1999 we were pregnant with Christopher, and we were calling each other cute names like MamaMaus and PapaScott. And when I started a site on the brand spaning new edithispage.com, it didn't take long for me to start calling it PapaScott, even after Christopher was born later that month. The name fits, even today.

So why PapaScott? Why not?
