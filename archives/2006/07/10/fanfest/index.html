Fanfest

10 Jul 2006

On Sunday Christopher and I left MamaMaus with her injured foot at home to check out the last day of the World Cup FanFest in Hamburg. In his Italia shirt he fit right in.

First he had to try out the trampoline.

Flying at the Heiligengeistfeld. (Update: I just realized that's my old office building in the background. Netlife RIP. The color is washed out because the sky was really dark, and I had to lighten up the picture to show Christopher's smile.)

It started to rain, so we went inside to the foosball tables.

When the weather cleared, we tried out the life-sized version.
