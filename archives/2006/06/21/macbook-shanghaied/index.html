MacBook Shanghaied

21 Jun 2006

My MacBook, ordered on 31 May from the German Apple Store, hasn't been delivered yet. But thanks to the Apple Tracking site at apecode, where one can track European Apple Store orders, I know my MacBook is out of the factory and waiting for a flight out of Shanghai. Expected delivery date: 29 June.

Four weeks from order to delivery? Next time I think I'll try a local dealer.
