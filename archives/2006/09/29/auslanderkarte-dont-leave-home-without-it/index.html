PapaScott And you know what they said? Well, some of it was true!

29 Sep 2006

Yesterday the German Interior Ministry announced the introduction of a personal id card for foreign residents, an Aufenthaltskarte similar the the Personalausweis issued to German citizens. I'm not so hot about all my personal data on a digitally readable smart card, but it will be practical to be able to leave my passport at home when I want to buy a phone or rent a video.
