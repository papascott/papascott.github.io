Technical Translation

11 Jan 2006

A friend of my sister-in-law is looking for a technical translator, and
she thought that perhaps a reader of PapaScott might be qualified and
interested in the job:

"Who is able to either correct a translation of a technical text from
German into English or to translate it totally on his own? The text to
be translated is the homepage of a German company (see
http://www.sintertechnik.com), and the translator should have some
knowledge in areas such as high performance technical ceramics (for
example ceramic bearings), development and processing of specific
ceramic powders to the pressing and firing of ceramical parts (like
spray-dried powders), as well as electroceramics (EMC- Electromagnetic
compatibility, VDR - voltage dependant resistors, multilayers, capacitors).

"If you think that you are the right person for this task, please
contact me at jurgen@schell.elcom.ru."
