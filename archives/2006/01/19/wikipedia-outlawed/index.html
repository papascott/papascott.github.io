Wikipedia Outlawed

19 Jan 2006

The newest trend in Germany seems to be to ban parts of the internet. After Heidi Klum's father and the Sozialgericht Bremen, someone in Berlin is trying to ban Wikipedia.

Of course, since Wikipedia is hosted in the US, the German courts don't have any jurisdiction. They did manage to put a temporary restraining order on German domain www.wikipedia.de to prevent it from redirecting to de.wikipedia.org.

Silly fools. Don't they realize that if Wikipedia is outlawed, only outlaws will have Wikipedia?
