Cooking with Spinach

31 Jan 2006

I'm sure he does it just to shock us, but Christopher regularly requests spinach for supper. He didn't learn to like it from his parents, neither of us can stand the stuff. Now the spokesperson for spinach in Germany is not Popeye, but the ex-model and ex-TV personality Verona Pooth (née Feldbusch), who in commercials said that her spinach is "the one that goes blubb" when it cooks, and even put out a spinach cookbook. Paris Hilton couldn't have said it any better.
