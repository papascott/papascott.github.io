Goma Burns

19 Jan 2002

Doc and Scoble have noted the scant news coverage given to the volcano eruption that has all but destroyed the city of Goma, Congo, leaving 500,000 people homeless. At least this evening on ZDF Heute it was the top story, with an appeal for donations.
