Backwards Progress

03 Jan 2002

This is a sad day for humanity. Christopher can now crawl out the crib by himself. Nobody is safe anymore. Nobody.

My brother Steve turns 36 today... Happy Birthday!

On the Euro front, my last DM coins are now gone, and my last outdated currency is a single 50 DM note. German banks are packed, much more than expected, full with people willing to wait hours to change all the cash in their wallet at once and be done with it.

I thought on New Years Day that we were over jet lag, but yesterday both Christopher and I were very out of sorts for our first day of work/day care. Today is better.

Is it just me, or are weblogs.com/blogtracker missing a lot of updated sites lately? For example. Adam Curry seems not to be showing up at all, and today's page flip for PapaScott was missed.
