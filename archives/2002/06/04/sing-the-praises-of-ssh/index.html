Sing the Praises of SSH

04 Jun 2002

Brent Ashley sings The Praises of SSH (in particular, OpenSSH), and Martin and Brent Simmons say "Amen". I can only add that running rsync (like scp, but faster and better) and cvs (version control) over ssh make it cooler yet, and that tramp for (x)emacs and the netrw functions in vim enable you to locally edit files on your ssh server.
