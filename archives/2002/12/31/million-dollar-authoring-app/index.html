Million Dollar Authoring App

31 Dec 2002

I was at first confused by Mark Pilgrim's use of the cite tag, but now that he has clarified that he's not doing it "for the children", but rather just for himself, I can see the point. What I need is a million dollar authoring app so I can keep track of all those million dollar tags I can use. I don't think a two bit bookmarklet is up to the job.

(And is it just me, or does Mark's million dollar markup prevent the Movable Type bookmarklet from working in my two-bit browser? Why, I'm surprised that anyone links to him at all. :-) Update Mark points out that it is just me.
