How Many Macs Run OSX?

18 Apr 2002

Here's a question for my readers who are Mac Men (or Mac Women): what percentage of actively used Macs run OSX? A fourth? A third? Half? I suspect my employer needs a OSX-capable machine to test our products, but I don't have any numbers to back up my suspicion. We're all Unix geeks who have no clue about Macs. Feel free to leave a comment.

Update: Thanks to Garret for the link, and welcome to a couple of new folks who left comments. I should also be a bit more specific. My employer sells server software for home banking and brokerage, so I'm really interested in how many folks would use OSX for home banking with a web browser (either with a login/password or a key stored on diskette or chipcard).
