First Steps

13 Mar 2002

The CeBIT starts today in not-too-distant Hannover. I'm glad not to be there. Meanwhile, Mama flies to Frankfurt for an impromptu afternoon meeting. Impromptu far-away meetings are never good.

Lots of people are saying how they are downloading Mozilla 0.9.9, but since my system has apt-get, I don't have to. My nightly apt-get cron job will do it for me. Heh.

"The (US) INS is the Mickey Mouse Club of federal agencies, but this... is an insult to Mickey Mouse." Mama always suspected this (it took them 4 tries to issue her a Green Card with her name spelled correctly), but now we know it's true.
