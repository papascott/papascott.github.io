Turkey Day

22 Nov 2001

No holiday here, just a normal work day, and no turkey. Just a glass (or two) this evening of Beaujolais Nouveau (known here as Beaujolais primeur).

A sobering work day today. A sysadmin knows in the back of her mind that the real threats to her network are internal, not external. But it's still a shock when that shadow in the back of her mind becomes real. I didn't choose this profession for the joy of spying on my co-workers. But my firm pays me, among other things, to protect its assets. But some parts of that job are no fun at all.
