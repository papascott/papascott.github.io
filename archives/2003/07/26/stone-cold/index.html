Stone Cold

26 Jul 2003

Listening to our local corporate 40 station this evening, something caught my ear that I had to listen more closely. Was the north German teeny band Echt now singing in English? No, the band Deuce Project has covered the bittersweet 'don't you wish your ex would come crawling back to you so you could turn your back on her' song 'Keine Liebe', which they translated as 'Stone Cold'. Interesting that an American band would cover a German song, and a fairly intelligent song at that. Typical that the cover band gets the credit for the text and the catchy melody.

Denn Du trägst keine Liebe in Dir,
nicht für mich und für irgendwen.
Du trägst keine Liebe in Dir,
Dir nachzutrauern hat keinen Sinn mehr.
Denn Du trägst keine Liebe in Dir.
Dich zu vergessen, war nicht sehr schwer.
Denn Du trägst keine Liebe in Dir.
