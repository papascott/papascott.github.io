Everywhere Overnight

12 Mar 2003

It seems everyone and his brother has mentioned that McDonald's is to Offer Wireless Internet. But does anyone read the fine print? The press release did not come from McDonald's, but from their wireless provider. This is a 3 month trial program. In 10 restaurants. Out of 20000. At a company that tests a hamburger for years before they roll it out. Do the math.
