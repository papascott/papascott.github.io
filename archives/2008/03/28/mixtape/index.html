28 Mar 2008

Mixwit (as seen at TechCrunch and Gigold) is a fun little toy to create virtual mixtapes for free (by searching for mp3 files on Seeqpod). You can also decorate a widget cassette tape where the wheels spin when the music plays. Cute.
