Twins Ad with Pat Neshek

18 Mar 2008

Twins pitcher and Minnesota native Pat Neshek has been keeping a weblog since he was in the minors. It's now spring training and Pat will be starting his second full year in the major leagues. The Twins now figure that Pat is well known enough to star in his own TV ad for the team. Well, maybe he's not as well known as they thought...

Pat himself blogged about the spot (but click fast, his hoster hasn't implemented permalinks), and if you're not from Minnesota you might need to Google to get the bit about the sideburns. All of the Twins TV ads are pretty good... the best one was last year with the Johan Santana/Joe Nathan carpool.
