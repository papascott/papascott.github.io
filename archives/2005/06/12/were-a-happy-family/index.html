We're A Happy Family

12 Jun 2005

The German Blogosphere is all a twitter about the new weblog network Spreeblick Verlag KG founded this weekend, with weblogs concentrating on music, games, poltics, culture, and broadcasting, with even a mention in tomorrows Focus magazine (as in dead-tree glossy paper). Does this mean that weblogs are selling out? No, I think that progress is good, and that any organization with a tag line from the Ramones must be off to a good start. Now we just need a second German weblog network, so we can have catfights just like Calcanis and Denton.
