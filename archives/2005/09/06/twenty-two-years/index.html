Twenty-Two Years

06 Sep 2005
We went down to the courthouse
and the judge put it all to rest
No wedding day smiles no walk down the aisle
No flowers no wedding dress

That was twenty-two years ago. Tonight we are getting together after work, leaving Christopher with his Oma, and driving off together to.... a parent-teacher meeting at the kindergarten that neither of us really wants to attend.

We really know how to celebrate our anniversary, don't we? Or not.
