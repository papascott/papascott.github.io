McCafe Elmshorn

17 Sep 2005

Today we visited a new McCafe in Elmshorn, northwest of Hamburg. It's a coffee bar inside a regular McDonald's restaurant, and the idea is relatively new in Germany, although it apparently hasn't gone over well in the US. Here they seem to be redoing the entire restaurant rather than creating a separate coffee area. In Elmshorn you sit on leather seats whether you are eating a Big Mac or drinking a Latte.
