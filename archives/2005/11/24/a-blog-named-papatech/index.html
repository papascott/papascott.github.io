A Blog Named PapaTech

OK, let's see if this works...

In an attempt to separate the exciting stuff from the boring stuff, I've moved all my tech-related posts to a separate weblog, PapaTech (running on Typo under Rails). You can decide for yourself whether you find the expat/personal stuff at PapaScott to be exciting, or the hip/technical stuff at PapaTech, or neither (in which case, why are you even reading this?).

I'm going to try to automatically put summaries of the PapaTech posts at PapaScott, so if things break, that means it's not working yet.
