PapaScott I don't claim to succeed at all this

06 Jul 2005

Mrs. T at AFOE points to a profile of Angela Merkel at Slate that Wahlbloggers in Germany might find interesting. The comparisons to Thatcher are inevitable, and reflect wishful thinking by US conservatives that Merkel is somehow like them (she's not). There are some minor mistakes; for example a Mädchen is no "Lady". And don't get tripped up when it says the CSU is "economically liberal"; that's an Americanism and means the opposite of what you think it does.
