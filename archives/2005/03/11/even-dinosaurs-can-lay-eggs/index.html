Even Dinosaurs Can Lay Eggs

11 Mar 2005

Loic Le Meur: T-Online Germany launches weblogs on TypePad! Now I know why Heiko's blog has been so quiet lately.

This is great news for weblogging in Germany, even for those of us who work for the competition. The Telekom may be an IT dinosaur, but T-Online is still the number one ISP in Germany (and in Europe). Blogs still haven't reached critical mass, blogs are starting to get some media attention, and a push from the magenta giant can't hurt. And maybe, since T-Online is a multi-national ISP, they can generate some trans-national multi-lingual European blogging magic...

Naaa.... dinosaurs have small brains, they can't think that fast.
