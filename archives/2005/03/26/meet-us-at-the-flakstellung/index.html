Meet Us at the Flakstellung

26 Mar 2005

Tonight is the annual Easter Fire here in town, the biggest event of the year. Like every year, I got a chuckle from the announcement that it would be held at the Flakstellung. There are no signs or anything, you just have to know that that's the hill outside of town where the Fl(ieger)a(bwehr)k(anone) (anti-aircraft artillery) was located during the war.

Or just wait until the bonfire is lit, and follow the smoke.
