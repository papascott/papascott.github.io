In Front of the Dakota

04 Dec 2005

It was 25 years ago Wednesday Thursday that John Lennon was killed. Has it really been that long? It's an event (like the Challenger explosion) that I remember vividly and that changed how I felt about myself and the world. I had just started college, I was working at the student radio station at the U of M. Ihad the Sunday morning shift, so I was on the air for the world wide minute of radio silence 6 days after his death. (via curry.com)
