Fasching, the morning of

19 Feb 2007

This morning our son disappeared, and in his place a Sams appeared! A Sams is a being with red hair, a short trunk-like nose, a wet suit and swim flippers, and magical blue "wish dots" on its face that can grant wishes. It speaks or sings mostly in rhyme and causes a great deal of chaos. It usually arrives on Saturday (Samstag).

It is the star of a series of children's books by Paul Maar and two films.
