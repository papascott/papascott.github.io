Soundtrack of Life

16 Feb 2007

I don't follow every meme that comes along, but the Soundtrack of Life that's going through our company seemed interesting, especially since I never listen to music at random. Here are the rules:

  1. Open your player player (iTunes, Winamp, Media Player, iPod etc.).
  2. Set it to "Shuffle/Random".
  3. Press "Play".
  4. For each answer write the song title that's being played.
  5. For each new question hit the "Next" button.
  6. Don't lie or pretend to be cool - just type the titles.
