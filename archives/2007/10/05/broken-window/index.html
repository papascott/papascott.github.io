Broken Window

05 Oct 2007

The October 3rd holiday was good for the restaurant (it was like an extra weekend in the middle of the week), but it wasn't so good for our indoor playland. A child kicked out one of the clear plastic panels, and we had to close it down for the rest of the day.

We will be getting rid of the playland when we remodel next month (we need the room for more seats), so it's not worth the bother to properly repair. As I was leaving yesterday, a carpenter was putting in a round wooden panel so that we could let kids back into the plastic box.
