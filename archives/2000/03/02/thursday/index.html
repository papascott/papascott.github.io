Deutschland Elbtunnelbohrer «Trude» ist zurück aus der Tiefe "Trude" is the largest drill bit in the world (14.2 m diameter, 2000 metric tons), digging a 4th tube for the congested Elbe Autobahn tunnel under Hamburg's harbor. Today, after nearly 2 miles and 2-1/2 years, it reached the surface on the north side of the Elbe. However, the tube will not pen for traffic for another 2 years. «Trude» is a girl's name, but in this case stands for «tief runter unter die Elbe» - deep down under the Elbe.
