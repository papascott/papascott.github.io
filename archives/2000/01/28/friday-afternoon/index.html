Friday Afternoon

28 Jan 2000

I came home Friday evening to find Grandma Ruth had gone home, and that both Mama and Christopher were tired (and just a little bit cranky). I sent them both to bed. It seems Mama misses out on her afternoon nap when Grandma visits.

No matter how old you get, there's nothing like being with your parents to make you feel childish again!

At work I've been setting up a test apache server running under FreeBSD. It's actually quite nice to work with!

For the first time in 10 years, the dollar costs more than 2 DM. Expressed in modern terms, the Euro since yesterday costs less than a dollar.

I’m typing this bit with the new Mozilla M13 under Linux. This Mozilla seems usable! Or am I just tired of Netscape “kill -9” Communicator 4? Even the highly styled array looks almost like it’s supposed to. Netscape under Linux is a style killer!
