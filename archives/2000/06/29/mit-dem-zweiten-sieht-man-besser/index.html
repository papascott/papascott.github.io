Mit dem Zweiten sieht man besser

29 Jun 2000

Euro 2000 semifinal: Italy 3 Holland 1 in penalties (0-0 after extra time) So much for offensive soccer. Italy got a red card early, but Holland missed two penalties in regular time and lost their nerve long before the shootout even started. (CNNSI.com story)

Italy will meet France in the final on Sunday. Then things are quiet until the World Cup in 2002. Qualifications begin for Germany in September.

Hamburg news A 6 year old boy was killed by a pit bull on an inner-city school playground during recess on Monday. The story is still in the headlines today.

After a slow start, the Expo in Hannover is up to 150,000 visitors per day. However, need over 300,000 per day to meet their projection of 40 million total.
