New Media Management

21 Dec 2004

It's now as good as official. With the new year I will be working as sys admin for New Media Management, part of the Ströer Gruppe and parent company of blogg.de, working for and with Nico Lumma. So it turns out that I am smart enough after all to get a job because of my weblog.

Nico and I have been telling each other for over a year that "wouldn't it be cool if we could somehow work together", and I am very happy that it has now worked out. I am looking very forward to working with Nico and his crew (Hi Thorte! Hi NicoB!). I'll be able to work around Christopher's schedule, but I'm sure that Dönerstag (translation: döner kebab on Thursday) will become a regular part of my weekly plan.
