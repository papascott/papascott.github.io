Last One Gone is a Rotten Egg!

14 May 2004

Press release from my employer: "The company is carrying on discussions with potential investors to secure continued financing. Only with a positive result can the liquidity and therefore the existance of the firm be achieved. Because the talks are in a critical phase, the board has decided to postpone the annual meeting scheduled for tomorrow." So is the time to leave a sinking ship when my feet start getting wet, or should I wait until the water is up to my neck?
