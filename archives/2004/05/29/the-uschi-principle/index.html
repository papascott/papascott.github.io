The Uschi Principle

29 May 2004

A couple of weeks ago one of my co-workers told me, "Well, your wife's no Uschi." I wasn't sure what he was talking about, but thanks to the Abendblatt, now I know. Uschis are women who are "blond, naive, helpless and successful", who are able to get men to do everything for them. A Verona Feldbusch type. And there's even a book on how to be a good Uschi. So I guess I can glad I don't have an Uschi (as in Tussi) for a wife.
