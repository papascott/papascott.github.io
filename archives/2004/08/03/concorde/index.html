Concorde

03 Aug 2004

Christopher's favorite website is the video page at Airbus, especially the Concorde's final landing. So it was only natural that he wanted a toy Concorde of his very own.

At German toy stores we could only find tiny models for collectors for 20, but on eBay we found a shop in Great Britain that sold a model by Corgi for around 12 (incl. postage). It arrived today.
