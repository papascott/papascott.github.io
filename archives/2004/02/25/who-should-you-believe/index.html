Who Should You Believe?

25 Feb 2004

Sunday is election day in Hamburg. If you believe the NDR, the race is neck and neck. If you believe the Springer press, the CDU has a clear lead. Mayor Beust has cancelled a TV debate with SPD candidate Mirow scheduled for this evening. Spiegel Online thinks Mirow's Kindergarten poster made him look silly.
