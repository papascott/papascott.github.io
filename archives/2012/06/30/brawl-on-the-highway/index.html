Brawl on the Highway

30 Jun 2012

We always thought life in our sleepy little village was pretty quiet, but think again! Freely translated from the Nordheide Wochenblatt (30 June 2012):

Brawl on the Highway: a driver goes bezerk over failure to yield

When reason fails, some people rather trust their fists. That was the case last Thursday afternoon. The result: an injury and a whole list of charges. A driver from Buchholz (51) nearly cut off another driver (59) while pulling out of a driveway in Jesteburg.
